Flavour Finder respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Usage Information

  • Recipe Preferences

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted third-party service provider. Industry-standard security measures include:

  • Encrypted Data Transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure Password Hashing
  • Row-Level Security Policies

5. Third-Party Services

  • Groq: For AI-powered recipe generation.
  • Unsplash: For recipe images.

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

Your account information is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-Out of Communications

Flavour Finder is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
